About

"Each Duduk has its specific world and life, and in the performer's hand it will tell the new tone, new life to his music......"

Hovsep Grigoryan

The master Hovsep Grigoryan -OMO- (1928-2004) dedicated over 50 years to preparing Armenian Folk Instruments: Duduk, Zurna, Shvi, and others.

Many people have sought the special original sound of his prepared Duduk, but ineffectually.

Arthur Grigoryan

The son of Hovsep Grigoryan, Arthur H. Grigoryan has been preparing musical instruments since 1997. Studying under his father, he has adopted the unique characteristics of these instruments.

Considering the best masters today, Arthur keeps the family traditions alive while also improving the instruments with new technologies.

And most of today's best dudukists play the musical instruments prepared exclusively by Arthur Grigoryan, including the world-wide favoured Djivan Gasparyan.
